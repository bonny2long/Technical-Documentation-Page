Introduction

The Bonny Game Console is your gateway to an immersive gaming experience. This guide will help you get started, from setting up your console to playing your first game.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a first-timer, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know.

Note: Ensure you have all the necessary components before you start.
Setup and Installation

Follow these steps to set up your Bonny Game Console

  • Unbox your console and all accessories
  • Place the console on a flat, stable surface near your TV.
  • Connect the power cable to the console and a power outlet.
  • Plug the HDMI cable into the console and your TV.
  • Turn on the console and follow the on-screen setup instructions.
HDMI: High-Definition Multimedia Interface

Ensure that your TV is set to the correct input to view the console's display.

Connecting to TV

Connecting your console to the TV is straightforward:

  • Locate the HDMI port on your TV.
  • Insert one end of the HDMI cable into the TV's HDMI port.
  • Connect the other end to the console's HDMI output.
  • Turn on both the TV and the console.
  • Select the appropriate HDMI input on your TV.

Once connected, you'll see the console's home screen on your TV.

Make sure the HDMI cable is securely connected to both devices.
Controller Configuration

To configure your controllers:

  • Turn on the console.
  • Press the sync button on the controller.
  • Press the sync button on the console to pair the controller.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

Once paired, your controller will be ready for use.

Tip: Charge your controller fully before starting the pairing process
Game Play

Now that your console is set up, you can start playing games:

  • Insert a game disc into the console or select a game from your library.
  • Use the controller to navigate the game menu.
  • Select 'Play' to start the game.
  • Follow the in-game tutorials if you're new to the game.
  • Enjoy your gaming experience!!!

If you encounter any issues, consult the troubleshooting section in the user manual.

Always save your game progress before turning off the console.
